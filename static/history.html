La Cité du Vin - Bordeaux

La Cité du Vin is an extraordinary cultural facility dedicated to the world of wine. Located in Bordeaux, France, this unique museum offers visitors a journey through the history, culture, and diversity of wine, from its ancient origins to its modern-day significance across the world.

What is La Cité du Vin?

La Cité du Vin is a place where visitors can experience the rich history and future of wine. This vibrant space invites you to explore wine culture, as well as the heritage of Bordeaux as a renowned wine-producing region.

What to Expect?

At La Cité du Vin, you'll find:

  • Interactive Exhibits: Engage with fascinating multimedia exhibits that explain the history and importance of wine in different cultures.
  • Tasting Rooms: Participate in tastings to explore the incredible range of wines from Bordeaux and other famous wine-producing regions of the world.
  • The Panoramic Bar: Enjoy a glass of wine with a spectacular view of Bordeaux and the Garonne River.

The Location

Located in the heart of Bordeaux, La Cité du Vin sits on the banks of the Garonne River, offering visitors breathtaking views of the city and the river. It's a must-see destination for wine enthusiasts, travelers, and anyone interested in the cultural heritage of Bordeaux.

Plan Your Visit

If you're planning to visit Bordeaux, don't miss the chance to experience La Cité du Vin! It's not only an exhibition space but also a place where you can immerse yourself in the world of wine.
