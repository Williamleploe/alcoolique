The Wine Making Process

Wine making is a delicate process that involves several steps, from harvesting grapes to fermentation and aging. Hereâ€™s an overview:

1. Harvesting

Grapes are carefully selected and harvested at the right time to ensure the best quality wine.

2. Crushing & Pressing

The harvested grapes are crushed to release their juice. Red wines keep the skins, while white wines are usually pressed immediately.

3. Fermentation

Yeast is added to convert sugars into alcohol. This process can take several days to weeks.

4. Aging & Maturation

Wines are aged in barrels or tanks to develop their flavors over time.

5. Bottling

Once the wine has reached the desired taste, it is bottled and prepared for distribution.
